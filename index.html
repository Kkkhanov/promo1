That is a really sad day for the UK! Bank of England sues Robert Peston for what he said on live TV. Every Briton should know about it!

  • Published
    9 hours ago
By Stuart Harratt & Andrew Barton & PA Media
BBC News
Read the official transcript from Robert Peston's interview below.

DELETED INTERVIEW WITH Robert Peston

Robert Peston : "Let me tell you something, you don't have to work to be rich. If you figure that out, you'll be well off."

Paul Brand: "Since there is a way to do it, why not share it with us?"

Robert Peston : "What's the point? I never get anything back for sharing my secrets. In any way, don't even try to find out how I earned my riches in the past 3 years. I won't reveal my secret to anyone, even if you offer me a lot. Otherwise, common Britons will never learn how to look for opportunities. More than that, if they find out how I do it, the whole country could suffer."

Paul Brand: "What harm would making money do to Britain?"

Robert Peston : "Think about it. If a man can become a millionaire in less than two months, why keep working? Sure, everyone can use my method, but who would work in restaurants, stores, or factories if everyone were wealthy?"

Paul Brand: "This sounds too good to be true. I have always believed you to be honest and sincere, but I don't believe you."

Robert Peston : "Did you just call me a liar? Huh, I'll prove it to you that any poor Brit can make thousands of dollars a day by just spending 10-20 minutes of free time a day. I'll show you how much I make doing so myself. Long story short, stocks have something to do with it, and everybody is getting rich on them right now."

Robert Peston pulled out his phone and opened some kind of app on it. Robert then handed the phone to the Paul Brand, who was shocked by what he saw.

Robert Peston : "How cool is that? I've only spent £200 to get started, and now this program is earning me tens of thousands a day, and it even works while I'm asleep."

Within seconds, the operator approached the Paul Brand and whispered something to him.

Mary Nightingale: "I just heard the camera captured the link you just showed me when you handed me your cell phone. The whole audience saw it."

Robert Peston's confusion was evident. It was now clear to everyone what he had been doing for so long.

Robert was confused. His secret was made public to everyone watching this program

Robert Peston : "A call from me will ensure that this issue doesn't air. You must cut the link you saw. I do not want to share it with anyone."

Paul Brand: "We're on live TV, so there's no need to hide it anymore. Maybe it's better to let common Britons learn what it's all about."

Robert Peston : "Yes, it's beyond my control now. Well, it has been three years since I started using the platform . All you have to do is register using this link and make a deposit of at least £200. This is the minimum amount required for the program to function properly. Once that's done, Once that's done, you just let the team of professional traders on the platform do the rest. Checking your balance and withdrawing your earnings is all you need to do."

Paul Brand: "Can you explain how this program works and teach us how to earn as much as you do?"

Paul Brand handed over his phone.

Robert Peston : "It does not require any skills or knowledge. If you give me your cell phone, I'll register you for Quantum FBC, and you'll personally find out how much you can earn after the interview."

Paul Brand handed Robert Peston his cell phone. It only took minutes for a personal account to be created, and a minimum deposit had already been made.

Paul Brand: "That's it? What should I do next?"

Robert Peston : "There is nothing else to do. We'll see how much money you'll earn in 20 minutes."

Paul Brand: "If it truly works, then what are Britons who don't have a minimum deposit amount supposed to do?"

Robert Peston : "When I learned about Immediate Growth, I didn't have any extra money, since we were going through a pandemic. Alternatively, you can use a credit card to fund your account. Money is useless if it does not work. Don't let this opportunity pass you by."

About 20 minutes later it was time to check the Paul Brand's balance.

Robert convinced everyone of his words

Robert Peston : "So, how much did you earn in those 20 minutes?"

Paul Brand: "I can't believe it! I earned £30 and now my balance is £405! Is it true?"

Paul Brand couldn't believe his eyes

Soon after, the Bank of England called the studio. Their fear caused them to demand that the live broadcast be halted immediately. Taking advantage of Quantum FBC would cause the banking system to lose customers. The only thing people would use their cards for would be withdrawals of their income from the platform. That isn't profitable for any bank.

Following the interruption, one of our editors decided to test this method for himself. He wrote a detailed report on his findings.

Day 1:

"My first impression was that it would not be that simple. Despite that, I really wanted to check it out. I had to use my credit card because I didn't have enough money to make the minimum deposit at the time of the investigation. I successfully invested £200 and waited to see what would happen.

When nothing happened, I thought I'd been duped. However, the algorithm started working after a few minutes. I was overjoyed, but then I saw the stats - my first trade was unprofitable by £22!
In the first couple minutes of working with this platform , there were some losses. But the next deal, as well as 4 after it, helped me gain more money. In a few minutes, my balance increased from £200 to 410!".

Day 2:

"The first thing I did in the morning was check my balance, which showed £814! In just one day, my balance doubled. Despite wanting to withdraw my profit, I decided to wait a week."

Day 7:

"Haven't checked my balance on the Quantum FBC platform all week. It was kind of difficult because I was afraid that my money was no longer there. As soon as I got back to my office, I saw that almost 85% of all deals were profitable. There were another 15% that didn't work. Despite that, it paid off. I now have £4230 on my account! I withdrew £3500 to buy a present for my wife. It all happened very fast, within an hour, and the rest of the money kept bringing me income."

Here is the bank statement:

"There's nothing better than Quantum FBC! If I hadn't withdrawn my profits, £200 would have turned into a million in 11 weeks."

Short instructions on how to start earning on Quantum FBC

  • 1. Use the link provided by Robert Peston. Kindly wait for a call from platform's manager to complete registration
  • 2. Top up your balance. The minimum deposit to start the program is £200.
  • 3. In a few minutes, the program will begin the transactions.
  • Withdrawal of money can be made at any time. It is credited to the account within © 2-3 hours (depending on the bank).
  • Up until 10.12.2023, registration of accounts will be free.
